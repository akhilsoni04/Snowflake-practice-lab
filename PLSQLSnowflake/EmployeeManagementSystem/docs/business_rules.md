## Department Rules
- Department must exist before assigning employees.

## Employee Hiring Rules
- Employee salary must be greater than 0.
- Employee email must be unique.
- Employee hire_date cannot be in the future.

## Salary Update Rules
- New salary must be greater than 0.
- Every salary update must be recorded in salary_history automatically.

## Termination Rules
- Employee record should not be deleted.
- Termination updates employee status to 'TERMINATED'.
